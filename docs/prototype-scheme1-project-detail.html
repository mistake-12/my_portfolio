Modular Speaker System

A modular speaker system designed for seamless integration into modern living spaces. This prototype shows layout rhythm, not final copy.

Role
Product design, interaction, visual direction
Tools
Rhino, KeyShot, Figma
Timeline
6 weeks, concept to prototype
Deliverables
CMF, form study, UI spec, motion notes

Overview

Problem, approach, outcome
Problem
Consumers want flexibility, but modular products often feel visually fragmented and hard to set up.
Approach
One consistent geometry and a repeatable joining system, plus a simple interaction model.
Outcome
A family of modules that can scale, with a clear setup flow and a coherent silhouette.
